Chicken Tikka Masala

One of my all time favorites! Chicken Tikka Masala is not as authentically Indian as you might think. It was invented by the Brittsh as a spin-off of a similar vegetarian Indian dish. Alas, you still get to use some delicious Indian spices!

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken thighs (you could use bone-in as well
  • Basmati Rice
  • Garam Masala spice
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Some other stuff

Here's how you make it kind of.

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Cook onions (I forgot to mention you need onions)
  3. Add spices
  4. Add cream and reduce
  5. Put it on some cooked rice
